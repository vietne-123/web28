China tightens border with Vietnam, strokes trade worries

Toughter controls cast shadow over Hanoi's economic recovery

TOMOYA ONISHI, NIKKEI staff wriiter

December 24, 2021 18:44

HANOI -- CHINA has tightened its land border with Vietnam amid worries over the omicron variant of COVID-19, dealing a blow to trade from the Southest Asian country as it battles to get its economy back on track in the face of the pandemic

Beijing informed Vietnam on thursday that foreign drivers would be barred from crossing the border between the two cocuntries from friday due to a request from Chinese health authorities, with similar measures also affecting China's border with Myanmar and Laos. China is Vietnam's second-largest export market and its biggest source of imports.
